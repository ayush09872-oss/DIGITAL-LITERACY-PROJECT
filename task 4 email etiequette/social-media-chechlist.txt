 Social Media Do's and Don'ts
 ✅ Do's
- Maintain a professional profile
- Use respectful and polite language
- Verify information before sharing
- Protect your personal information
- Use strong passwords and privacy settings

 ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share sensitive personal details
- Avoid posting inappropriate content
- Do not engage in online arguments or hate speech
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links
- Do not spread fake or unverified news
