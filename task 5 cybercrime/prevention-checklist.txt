Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone
- Do not approve unknown payment requests
- Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or companies
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
- Use official apps for transactions only
- Enable app locks and two-factor authentication
- Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history
- Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store

Financial Safety (UPI Specific)
- Remember: You do NOT need to enter UPI PIN to receive money
- Double-check payment requests before approving them
 Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
